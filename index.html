Frequently asked questions
Will this create work for us?
No. Second Nature is a fixed monthly programme with no carbon audit and no ongoing admin required from your team.
Is this carbon offsetting?
No. Second Nature is not a carbon offset or credit scheme. We do not use carbon neutral or net zero claims.
What happens if participation stops?
If a business is no longer active, its public page reflects that status. Active participation is required for visibility within the programme.
Is this a substitute for improving our operations?
No. Second Nature supports UK conservation alongside your operations. It does not replace operational improvements.
